All About Us

Health Locker is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company with over 3 decades of excellence as an industrial solution provider, has diversified in mining, cement textile and chemical industries with almost 2000+ customer base. Health Locker has spreaded wings all over Rajasthan with 6 offices. We are offering a range of industrial solutions from oil to capital equipements to all customers with a team of over 50+ committed family members, we have become a synonym for trust and reliability. We constantly deliver the best experience to our customers. Over the years, we have persistently evolved to become one of India's leading industrial solution providers. Our spirit to challenge convention and think differently has pioneered several brand business successes.

We represent industry leaders such as Shell Lubricants, SKF Lincoln, Xylem India, Victaulic Couplings, Atlas Copco Air Compressor, EWAC Alloys, Karcher Cleaning Systems and many more as our exclusive channel partner for Rajasthan.
